PROJECTS AT A GLANCE

2018-16

Free Training

MG Foundation conducted free IT and English speaking classes for the underprivileged candidates at G. C. Group of Institutions, Computer Science Institute, Answers Computers, & Rishika Computer Institute & IT Services in Jaipur.

2018-16

Free Book Distribution

As part of the Social Activity, MG Foundation distributed books for the subjects of English and Computer to more than 120 underprivileged candidates of different areas in Jaipur to help them to learn the basics of the same.
