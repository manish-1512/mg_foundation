Education

We know that some people are educated but do not have enough skills to earn money that’s why we are providing skill development classes with
the help of PMKVY and many other organization.


MG Foundation knows that education plays a great role in everyone’s life as it brings a positive effect on human life. There are many organization dedicating to improving education and every organization has a specific focus and right now we are focusing on the modern concept of education. The modern concept of education mainly focuses on developing skills with education. The modern concept is the progressive way of imparting education which concentrates on the overall development of a person. It prepares an individual to face the challenges of the world and aims at making him independent and self-reliant.

Poverty

Poverty makes people unable to get proper education, food, shelter, clothing, and many other things because of the lack of money. While tackling the issues that poverty has given rise to, ‘charity’ alone is not a lasting solution to enable change for the millions of People living in India today, who have no access to food or education and are exploited daily. MG Foundation works to save lives, defeat poverty and achieve social justice.
