Who we are

MG Foundation is a Non-Governmental charitable Organisation (NGO) who work in many fields in India for the growth and development for the ever one. The whole management of the organization is handled by the MG team. A group of young corporate professionals came together in 2019 to set up MG Foundation to work with grassroots initiatives for effecting positive changes in the lives of underprivileged children, their families
and communities.

We believe in making a small difference in the lives of every human being who is less privileged. We are Not being a very traditional
organization, Mg foundation is run by a very young, dynamic and energetic team who are always open to learning new ways to make the
world a better place to stay in.

MG Foundation has evolved Seven working models namely Education, Health, Food Poverty, Malnutrition, Sanitation, Women
Empowerment.

Mission

  • Being a catalyst in the ongoing positive programs including but not limited to
    Education, Health and Food, that directly improve and transform the lives of the people.
  • Working in partnership to create, find and support diverse segments of the welfare
    programs to secure and protect the rights of the people through equitable opportunity
    and inclusive development efforts.
